Mixed Martial Arts Waterford

MMA is waterfords newest Mixed Martial Arts club, located in Waterford Business Park it is easily accessible by all in the south east. We offer a rage of different Classes to suit everbody's needs. if you wish to make an enquiry please feel free to Contact Us. We are open 6 Days a week running classes for kids from 4 upwards, teenagers from 13 upwards, women only classes and mixed classes. We offer expertise many different martial art forms and you can become a member of our club gaining you access to our members only section.
